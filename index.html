NSBikeRide

Bikes with Nerds

Come join other developers who made the pilgrimage to San Francisco for WWDC for a bike ride. Whether you have a WWDC ticket or not, you're welcome to join us.

Get your tickets here Now!

When?

We shall ride at 3pm June 1st.

Where?

We shall meet in front of Moscone West. Should be easy to find :)

Bikes?

It's fine if you don't want to bring your bike with you through on the airplane. If you will need to rent a bike, contact me right now so I can give the rental people a count. The week of WWDC is apparently also the week of a big time triathlon, so the sooner we can reserve bikes the better.

Lycra?

Sure, if you want to. We'll ride for an hour or so, then stop for food and/or coffee. We just want you to be comfortable, so wear whatever does that for you.

But I have Questions!

No problem sir or madame. Just contact me on twitter or App.net under the name @samuelgoodwin and ask away.
